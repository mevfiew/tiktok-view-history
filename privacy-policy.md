# Privacy Policy — TikTok View History

**Last updated:** April 17, 2026

## Overview

TikTok View History is a browser extension that tracks TikTok videos you view while browsing tiktok.com. Your privacy is fundamental to this extension's design.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, transmit, or share any data.** Period.

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API. No data ever leaves your device.

## What is stored locally

When you browse tiktok.com, the extension captures and stores the following information in your browser:

- Video ID and URL
- Thumbnail image URL
- Caption text (truncated to 500 characters)
- Creator's username and display name
- Sound / audio track name
- Hashtags parsed from the video
- Video duration in seconds
- Timestamp of when you viewed it

## Data sharing

None. Zero. There are no servers, no analytics, no third-party services. The extension does not make any network requests of its own.

## Data retention

Data is stored until you delete it. You can:
- Delete individual videos from your history
- Clear all history at once from the extension popup
- Uninstall the extension, which removes all stored data

The extension stores a maximum of 10 000 items. Oldest items are automatically removed when this limit is reached.

## Permissions

- **storage** — To save your view history locally in Chrome
- **host_permissions (tiktok.com)** — To run the content script that captures viewed videos on tiktok.com
